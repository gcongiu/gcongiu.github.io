Dr. Giuseppe Congiu is a Research Scientist at Innovative Computing Laboratory
(ICL), University of Tennessee Knoxville. Before joining ICL he was a
Postdoctoral Fellow at Argonne National Laboratory (ANL) where he worked on
Parallel Programming Models and Runtime Systems for large scale Supercomputing
Clusters. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Johannes Gutenber
Univerisity Mainz, Germany, and B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic
Engineering from the University of Cagliari, Italy. From 2011 to 2013 he was
the recipient of a Marie Curie Initial Training Network scholarship from the
European Commission. He has published several papers in the context of
distributed I/O and runtime systems and given several talks on the subject
matter.
